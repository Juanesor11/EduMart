About EduMart

We are a group of students passionate about making school more affordable and sustainable. We noticed that several of our peers were having difficulty purchasing expensive workbooks and some of their uniforms, so we built a marketplace specifically for our school community. Thanks to EduMart, students can buy and sell used workbooks and uniforms, as well as find and offer tutoring at reasonable prices, reducing paper waste through recycling and allowing students in need to earn extra money.

Mission

Our mission is to enable students to have the required materials for classes at a more affordable price and in a sustainable way. In addition, we want students to have better grades in school.

Vision

Our goal is to be recognized at school. We want our service to be useful, standing out and marking a before and after in the school's book distribution system and fostering a sense of community.

Values

  • Accessibility
  • Trust
  • Security
  • Sustainability
  • Community
